Third-Party Services

This site loads resources from the following third-party CDNs. By visiting, your browser makes requests directly to these services. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • GitHub Pages — This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log visits per their Privacy Statement.

I do not have access to any data these services collect, nor do I share data with them beyond what is technically required to load the page.
